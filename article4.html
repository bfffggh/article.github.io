Bulbasaurs

The Bulbasaurs have garlics on their backs, this helps them use Sleep powder.
They are also designed to eat plants, and can even climb on 90° surfaces.
They use this for defense against predators, and their bodies grow from garlics (bulbs)
If a bulbasaur's body fails to grow from a bulb, 8 more bulbs launch-
into the air in search of their new growing place. When they find dirt,-
they start growing.
If a bulbasaur's body grows successfully, the complete Bulbasaur-
gets teleported to it's parents. The remaining 8 bulbs will launch-
into the air in search of their new growing place. When they find dirt,-
They grow, but only if two bulbasaur give it their pollen.
